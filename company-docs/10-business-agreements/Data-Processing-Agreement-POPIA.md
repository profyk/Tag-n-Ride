## 3. OBLIGATIONS OF THE OPERATOR

The Operator undertakes to:

### 3.1 Lawful Processing
- Process personal information only on **documented instructions** from Tag-n-Ride
- Not process personal information for any purpose beyond the Master Agreement
- Immediately inform Tag-n-Ride if any instruction infringes POPIA

### 3.2 Security Measures (POPIA s19)
- Implement appropriate technical and organisational measures including:
  - [ ] Encryption of data in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256)
  - [ ] Access controls and privileged access management
  - [ ] Regular penetration testing (at least annually)
  - [ ] Security awareness training for all staff with access
  - [ ] Incident detection and response procedures

### 3.3 Confidentiality
- Ensure all personnel authorised to process personal information are bound by confidentiality obligations
- Provide access on a **strict need-to-know basis**

### 3.4 Sub-operators
- Not engage any sub-operator to process personal information without **prior written consent** from Tag-n-Ride
- If consent is granted, impose equivalent data protection obligations on sub-operators
- Remain fully liable to Tag-n-Ride for the acts of any sub-operator

### 3.5 Data Subject Rights (POPIA s23)
- Assist Tag-n-Ride in responding to data subject access requests, correction requests, and objection notices within **5 business days** of receiving the request

### 3.6 Data Breach Notification (POPIA s22)
- Notify Tag-n-Ride's Information Officer in writing within **24 hours** of becoming aware of any compromise
- Include in the notification: nature of the breach, categories and estimated number of data subjects affected, likely consequences, measures taken or proposed
- Cooperate fully with Tag-n-Ride's incident response and the Information Regulator investigation

### 3.7 Deletion and Return
- At the end of the Master Agreement, either delete or return all personal information to Tag-n-Ride within **30 days**, as instructed
- Provide written certification of deletion upon request

### 3.8 Audit Rights
- Allow Tag-n-Ride to conduct (or commission a third party to conduct) audits of the Operator's data processing activities on **30 days' notice** (annually, or after any breach)

## 4. CROSS-BORDER TRANSFERS

The Operator shall **not** transfer personal information outside the Republic of South Africa unless:
- Tag-n-Ride gives prior written consent, and
- The receiving country has adequate data protection laws equivalent to POPIA, or
- An appropriate safeguard is in place (binding corporate rules, standard contractual clauses)

---

## 5. TAG-N-RIDE'S OBLIGATIONS

- Provide lawful instructions only
- Ensure data subjects are informed of processing in Tag-n-Ride's Privacy Notice
- Maintain records of processing activities

## 6. LIABILITY AND INDEMNITY

6.2 Operator is liable for any breach of its obligations under this DPA.
6.3 Each party indemnifies the other against fines, penalties, and third-party claims arising from that party's own non-compliance with POPIA.

## 7. TERM
