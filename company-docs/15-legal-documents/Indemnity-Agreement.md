## PART 1: DEFINITIONS

**"Claim"** means any claim, demand, action, proceeding, investigation, liability, loss, cost, expense, penalty, fine, damage, or judgment of any nature, whether made or threatened, arising out of or in connection with the subject matter of the indemnity described herein.

**"Covered Event"** means the specific event, act, omission, or circumstance in respect of which the indemnity is provided, as described in clause 2.1.

**"Excluded Conduct"** means:

(a) Gross negligence on the part of Tag-n-Ride or its employees, officers, or agents;

(b) Wilful misconduct or fraud committed by Tag-n-Ride;

(c) Any act or omission that a court of competent jurisdiction finds to have been solely caused by Tag-n-Ride.

**"Indemnified Costs"** means all reasonable costs and expenses incurred by Tag-n-Ride in connection with a Claim, including legal fees on the attorney-and-own-client scale, court costs, expert fees, and settlement amounts.

**"Indemnified Parties"** means Tag-n-Ride, its directors, officers, employees, agents, successors, and assigns.

**"Indemnifier"** means the party providing the indemnity as identified above.

**"Liability Cap"** means the maximum amount payable by the Indemnifier under this Agreement, as set out in clause 3.

## PART 2: SCOPE OF INDEMNITY

### 2.1 Indemnity Obligation

Subject to the limitations and exclusions set out in this Agreement, the Indemnifier hereby irrevocably and unconditionally indemnifies, defends, and holds harmless the Indemnified Parties from and against all Claims arising from:

2.1.1 *[Select and customise applicable grounds:]*

(a) **Operational Indemnity:** Any act or omission of the Indemnifier, its employees, agents, subcontractors, or sub-operators in connection with the Underlying Agreement or the use of the Tag-n-Ride Platform;

(b) **Regulatory Non-Compliance:** The Indemnifier's failure to comply with any Applicable Laws, including the National Land Transport Act 5 of 2009, FICA, POPIA, or any regulatory licence condition applicable to the Indemnifier;

(c) **Third-Party Claims:** Any claim made by a third party (including passengers, regulators, or other operators) against Tag-n-Ride arising from the Indemnifier's conduct or operations;

(d) **Breach of Agreement:** The Indemnifier's breach of any term of the Underlying Agreement or these provisions;

(e) **IP Infringement:** Any claim that materials, data, or information provided by the Indemnifier to Tag-n-Ride infringes the intellectual property rights of any third party;

(f) **Fraud:** Any fraudulent act committed by the Indemnifier, its employees, Drivers, or agents in connection with the Platform;

(g) **Data Breach:** Any personal information security compromise caused by the Indemnifier's failure to comply with its POPIA obligations.

2.1.2 The indemnity in clause 2.1 includes, without limitation:

- All damages, losses, and penalties awarded by a court or arbitrator against Tag-n-Ride;
- All Indemnified Costs incurred by Tag-n-Ride in defending, settling, or investigating any Claim;
- Regulatory fines or penalties imposed on Tag-n-Ride as a direct result of the Indemnifier's conduct.

### 2.2 Exclusions from Indemnity

The indemnity in clause 2.1 does not apply to any Claim arising from:

2.2.1 Excluded Conduct (as defined above);

2.2.2 A Claim that a court of competent jurisdiction has finally determined arose solely and directly from Tag-n-Ride's own negligence without any contributing act by the Indemnifier;

2.2.3 Any Claim that has been fully settled or compromised without the Indemnifier's written consent (except in circumstances of urgency or where the Indemnifier has failed to respond within a reasonable period after notice).

## PART 4: CLAIMS PROCEDURE

### 4.1 Notification

4.1.1 If Tag-n-Ride becomes aware of any Claim in respect of which it intends to seek indemnification, it shall notify the Indemnifier in writing as soon as reasonably practicable, and in any event within thirty (30) days of becoming aware of the Claim.

4.1.2 The notification shall include:

(a) A description of the nature of the Claim;

(b) The amount claimed (if known);

(c) Copies of any relevant documents, notices, or correspondence.

4.1.3 Failure to provide timely notice shall not affect Tag-n-Ride's right to indemnification, except to the extent that the Indemnifier suffers material prejudice as a direct result of the delay.

### 4.2 Indemnifier's Right to Defend

4.2.1 Upon receipt of notice of a Claim, the Indemnifier may, on written notice to Tag-n-Ride within fifteen (15) Business Days, elect to assume control of the defence of the Claim at the Indemnifier's own cost.

4.2.2 Where the Indemnifier assumes control of a defence:

(a) Tag-n-Ride shall co-operate reasonably with the Indemnifier in the defence;

(b) Tag-n-Ride may appoint its own counsel at its own cost to observe the proceedings;

(c) The Indemnifier shall not settle any Claim in a manner that admits liability on Tag-n-Ride's behalf, requires Tag-n-Ride to take or refrain from any action, or imposes any obligation on Tag-n-Ride, without Tag-n-Ride's prior written consent.

### 4.3 Tag-n-Ride's Right to Act

If the Indemnifier does not elect to assume the defence within fifteen (15) Business Days of notice, Tag-n-Ride may take such steps to defend or settle the Claim as it deems appropriate, and the Indemnifier shall be bound by the outcome and shall reimburse all Indemnified Costs.

### 4.4 Recovery

Tag-n-Ride shall take reasonable steps to mitigate any Claim. If Tag-n-Ride recovers any amounts from a third party in respect of a Claim for which the Indemnifier has already paid indemnification, Tag-n-Ride shall promptly account to the Indemnifier for the net amount recovered (after deducting all reasonable costs of recovery).

## PART 5: SURVIVAL AND DURATION

### 5.1 Duration

This Agreement shall remain in force for the duration of the Underlying Agreement and for a period of three (3) years after its termination or expiry ("**Survival Period**"), in respect of any Claims arising from events occurring during the term of the Underlying Agreement.

### 5.2 Survival

The obligations of the Indemnifier under this Agreement survive the termination or expiry of the Underlying Agreement and of this Agreement itself in respect of Claims notified before the expiry of the Survival Period.
