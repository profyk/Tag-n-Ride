## RECITALS

A. The Principal Debtor has entered into [describe the underlying agreement — e.g., "an Operator Onboarding Agreement" / "a Fleet Owner Agreement" / "a commercial contract"] with Tag-n-Ride, reference number [AGREEMENT REF], dated [DATE] (the "**Underlying Agreement**"), in terms of which the Principal Debtor has incurred or may incur financial obligations to Tag-n-Ride;

B. As a condition of Tag-n-Ride entering into the Underlying Agreement with the Principal Debtor, Tag-n-Ride requires that the Surety provides a personal or corporate surety and guarantee in respect of the Principal Debtor's obligations;

## PART 1: DEFINITIONS

**"All Amounts Due"** means all money that the Principal Debtor owes or may in future owe to Tag-n-Ride under the Underlying Agreement or any related agreement, including:

(a) Principal amounts due for Services rendered;

(b) Platform fees and transaction charges;

(c) Interest at the agreed or prescribed rate;

(d) Legal costs on the attorney-and-client scale;

(e) Costs of enforcement of the Underlying Agreement;

(f) Any other amount, charge, or penalty lawfully owing.

**"Guarantee Limit"** means the maximum amount for which the Surety is liable under this Deed, as set out in clause 3.1.

**"Insolvency Event"** means the sequestration, winding-up, business rescue, liquidation, dissolution, or placement under curatorship of the Principal Debtor, or any analogous event.

**"Underlying Agreement"** means the agreement between the Principal Debtor and Tag-n-Ride described in the Recitals, and any amendments or extensions thereof.

---

## PART 2: SURETY AND GUARANTEE

### 2.1 Binding Surety

The Surety hereby binds himself/herself/itself as surety and co-principal debtor with the Principal Debtor to Tag-n-Ride for the due and punctual payment and performance by the Principal Debtor of All Amounts Due and all obligations under the Underlying Agreement.

### 2.2 Co-Principal Debtor

2.2.1 The Surety binds himself/herself/itself as co-principal debtor (*medekontraktant*) for all the obligations of the Principal Debtor under the Underlying Agreement.

2.2.2 This means that Tag-n-Ride may proceed against the Surety as if the Surety were the Principal Debtor, without first being required to proceed against the Principal Debtor.

### 2.3 Nature of Surety

This surety is:

2.3.1 **Continuing** — it covers all amounts due now and in the future under the Underlying Agreement, including amounts arising from renewals or extensions;

2.3.2 **Unconditional** — it is not subject to any condition other than the conditions expressly stated herein;

2.3.3 **Irrevocable** during the period described in clause 4.

---

## PART 3: GUARANTEE LIMIT

### 3.1 Maximum Liability

The Surety's maximum aggregate liability under this Deed is limited to:

**R [AMOUNT] (South African Rand [AMOUNT IN WORDS])**

plus all interest, legal costs, and enforcement costs as described in the definition of All Amounts Due.

### 3.2 No Reduction

The Guarantee Limit shall not be reduced by any partial payment made by the Principal Debtor unless and until the total outstanding balance of All Amounts Due falls below the Guarantee Limit.

---

## PART 4: CALL ON GUARANTEE

### 4.1 Demand Procedure

4.1.1 Tag-n-Ride may call upon this guarantee at any time that All Amounts Due (or any portion thereof) remain unpaid after the applicable payment due date under the Underlying Agreement.

4.1.2 To make a call, Tag-n-Ride shall deliver a written demand to the Surety:

(a) Specifying the amount claimed;

(b) Confirming that the amount is due and owing by the Principal Debtor;

(c) Demanding payment within [15 / 30] Business Days of the date of the demand.

### 4.2 Entitlement to Enforce

4.2.1 Tag-n-Ride may enforce this guarantee without first:

(a) Demanding payment from the Principal Debtor;

(b) Taking proceedings against the Principal Debtor;

(c) Exhausting remedies against the Principal Debtor or any other security held by Tag-n-Ride.

4.2.2 The Surety's liability is independent of and not affected by:

(a) Any failure or refusal by the Principal Debtor to perform its obligations;

(b) Any arrangement, compromise, or settlement between Tag-n-Ride and the Principal Debtor;

(c) Any release of any other surety;

(d) The dissolution, insolvency, or business rescue of the Principal Debtor.

---

## PART 5: PRINCIPAL DEBTOR OBLIGATIONS

### 5.1 Obligations Covered

The Surety acknowledges that the obligations of the Principal Debtor covered by this Deed include:

5.1.1 Payment of all platform fees, transaction charges, and other amounts owing under the Underlying Agreement;

5.1.2 Compliance with all KYC, FICA, and POPIA obligations under the Underlying Agreement;

5.1.3 Return of any QR Code Devices or equipment upon termination;

5.1.4 Payment of any penalties, chargebacks, or damages arising from the Principal Debtor's breach.

### 5.2 Notification

The Surety agrees to notify Tag-n-Ride immediately if the Surety becomes aware of any event that may affect the Principal Debtor's ability to perform its obligations, including insolvency proceedings or material financial distress.

---

## PART 6: WAIVERS BY THE SURETY

The Surety hereby expressly, irrevocably, and unconditionally waives:

### 6.1 Waiver of Benefit of Excussion (*Beneficium Excussionis*)

The Surety waives the right to insist that Tag-n-Ride first proceed against the Principal Debtor or exhaust its remedies against the Principal Debtor before proceeding against the Surety.

### 6.2 Waiver of Division (*Beneficium Divisionis*)

Where there are multiple sureties, the Surety waives the right to insist that Tag-n-Ride divide its claim between all sureties proportionally.

### 6.3 Waiver of Cession of Actions (*Beneficium Cedendarum Actionum*)

The Surety waives the right to demand that Tag-n-Ride first cede its rights of action against the Principal Debtor to the Surety before proceeding against the Surety.

### 6.4 Waiver of Notice of Default

The Surety waives any right to receive a prior notice of default from Tag-n-Ride before a demand is made under this Deed.

---

## PART 7: EFFECT ON SURETY'S OBLIGATIONS

### 7.1 Obligations Not Affected

The Surety's obligations under this Deed shall not be affected, reduced, or discharged by:

7.1.1 Any time or indulgence granted by Tag-n-Ride to the Principal Debtor;

7.1.2 Any variation, amendment, or waiver of the terms of the Underlying Agreement;

7.1.3 Any release, compounding, or dealing with any other surety or security;

7.1.4 Any failure by Tag-n-Ride to take, perfect, or enforce any other security;

7.1.5 The death, incapacity, sequestration, or dissolution of the Principal Debtor or any other surety;

7.1.6 Any novation, rescission, or amendment of the Underlying Agreement, unless such amendment is expressly agreed to by the Surety in writing.

### 7.2 Continuing Guarantee

This Deed creates a continuing guarantee and shall remain in full force and effect until all of the Principal Debtor's obligations under the Underlying Agreement have been irrevocably discharged in full.

---

## PART 8: INTEREST

If the Surety fails to pay any amount demanded under clause 4.1 by the due date, interest shall accrue on the overdue amount at the South African prime lending rate plus two percent (2%) per annum, calculated daily from the due date to the date of actual payment.

---

## PART 9: DURATION AND RELEASE

### 9.1 Duration

This Deed shall remain effective from the date of signature until:

9.1.1 All Amounts Due under the Underlying Agreement have been paid in full; and

9.1.2 The Underlying Agreement has been terminated or expired and all obligations thereunder fulfilled.

### 9.2 Release

Upon written confirmation from Tag-n-Ride that all obligations of the Principal Debtor have been discharged in full, this Deed shall be released and the Surety's obligations shall terminate.

---

## PART 10: GENERAL

### 10.1 Governing Law

This Deed is governed by the laws of the Republic of South Africa, including the common law principles applicable to suretyship.

### 10.2 Jurisdiction

The Surety consents to the jurisdiction of the High Court of South Africa (Gauteng Division, Pretoria) and the Magistrates' Court having jurisdiction over the Surety's domicilium citandi et executandi.

### 10.3 Domicilium

The Surety chooses the address stated on the cover page as its *domicilium citandi et executandi* for all purposes relating to this Deed. Any change of domicilium requires written notice to Tag-n-Ride.

### 10.4 Entire Agreement

This Deed constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties regarding suretyship and supersedes all prior discussions.

### 10.5 Severability

If any provision is found to be invalid, it shall be severed and the remaining provisions shall continue.

### 10.6 No Waiver

No failure to enforce any provision shall constitute a waiver.
