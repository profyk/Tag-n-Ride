## PART 4: FULL AND FINAL SETTLEMENT

### 4.1 Release upon Payment

Upon receipt of the Settlement Amount in full by the Receiving Party:

4.1.1 Each Party hereby unconditionally and irrevocably releases and discharges the other Party from all Released Claims;

4.1.2 Neither Party shall institute any further proceedings or make any further claim against the other Party in respect of any matter forming part of the Dispute;

4.1.3 Any pending legal proceedings between the Parties in respect of the Dispute shall be withdrawn by consent, with each Party bearing its own costs, by filing the appropriate withdrawal notice with the relevant court or arbitral tribunal within [5 / 10] Business Days of receipt of the full Settlement Amount.

### 4.2 Scope of Release

The release in clause 4.1 extends to:

4.2.1 All claims that were raised in the Dispute;

4.2.2 All claims that could have been raised in the Dispute but were not;

4.2.3 Any claims against the other Party's directors, officers, employees, and agents in respect of the subject matter of the Dispute.

### 4.3 Reservation of Rights

The release in clause 4.1 does not extend to:

4.3.1 Obligations created by this Agreement;

4.3.2 Any claims arising from events after the Effective Date;

4.3.3 Any claims of a different nature not forming part of the Dispute, as specifically identified in Schedule B (if applicable).

## PART 5: DEFAULT ON PAYMENT PLAN

### 5.1 Default

If the Paying Party fails to pay any instalment by its due date under the Payment Plan and does not remedy such default within five (5) Business Days of written notice:

5.1.1 The full outstanding balance of the Settlement Amount becomes immediately due and payable;

5.1.2 Interest shall accrue on the outstanding balance at the South African prime lending rate plus two percent (2%) per annum from the date of default to the date of payment;

5.1.3 Tag-n-Ride may, at its election, reactivate any suspended legal proceedings or institute new proceedings to enforce this Agreement.

### 5.2 Consent to Judgment

*[Include where agreed by the Parties:]*

The Paying Party consents to judgment being entered against it in the High Court of South Africa for the balance of the Settlement Amount in the event of default, in accordance with this clause.

### 6.2 Scope of Confidentiality

6.2.3 Any term of this Agreement.

### 6.3 Permitted Disclosures

Disclosure may be made to:

6.3.1 Legal advisers, accountants, or auditors who are bound by equivalent confidentiality obligations;
