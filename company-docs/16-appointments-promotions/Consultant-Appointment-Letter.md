### 5.1 Assignment

5.1.2 The Consultant hereby irrevocably assigns to Tag-n-Ride all right, title, and interest in and to all Work Product, including all copyright, patent rights, trade secrets, and other IP rights, globally, in perpetuity.

5.1.3 The Consultant agrees to execute any further documents and take any further steps reasonably required by Tag-n-Ride to give effect to this assignment.

### 5.2 Moral Rights

5.2.1 The Consultant waives any moral rights ("*droits moraux*") in respect of the Work Product to the fullest extent permitted under the Copyright Act 98 of 1978 and applicable law.

5.3.2 The Consultant must disclose all pre-existing IP incorporated in the Work Product to Tag-n-Ride's contact.

## 7. INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR STATUS

### 7.1 No Employment Relationship

7.1.1 The Consultant is engaged as an independent contractor and not as an employee of Tag-n-Ride.

7.1.2 This engagement does not create an employment relationship, and Tag-n-Ride has no obligation to:

(a) Deduct employees' tax (PAYE) from payments to the Consultant;

(b) Make contributions to the Unemployment Insurance Fund (UIF) on the Consultant's behalf;

(c) Make contributions to the Compensation Fund (COIDA);

(d) Provide leave, sick pay, bonuses, or any other employment benefits.

7.1.3 The Consultant is solely responsible for their own income tax obligations and must register as a provisional taxpayer with SARS if required, and must submit annual income tax returns in respect of consulting income.

### 7.2 Tax Obligations

7.2.1 The Consultant acknowledges their personal obligation to register for and pay income tax on consulting income under the Income Tax Act 58 of 1962.

7.2.2 Tag-n-Ride may be required to deduct **Employees' Tax (PAYE)** if the South African Revenue Service determines that this engagement constitutes a "personal service provider" or employment relationship in terms of the Fourth Schedule to the Income Tax Act, regardless of the label attached by the Parties. The Consultant indemnifies Tag-n-Ride against any tax shortfall or SARS penalty arising from the Consultant's failure to comply with their tax obligations.

7.2.3 Where the Consultant is a VAT vendor, the Consultant must charge VAT on invoices and submit VAT returns to SARS.

### 7.3 No Exclusive Engagement

The Consultant is free to provide services to other clients, provided this does not conflict with the Consultant's obligations under this letter or the NDA.
