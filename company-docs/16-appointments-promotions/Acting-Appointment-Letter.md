6.1 You are expected to perform the duties of the [ACTING ROLE TITLE] to the same standard as the permanent incumbent.

6.2 You will maintain the confidentiality of all information accessed in the acting role. Information accessed by virtue of the acting role may not be retained, used, or disclosed after the acting period concludes.

6.3 You remain bound by all policies and the Code of Conduct of the Company.

6.4 You will conduct a handover to [ABSENT EMPLOYEE NAME] upon their return, including a written summary of key decisions taken, matters in progress, and any issues requiring follow-up.
