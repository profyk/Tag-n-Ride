## LEGAL REQUIREMENTS BEFORE DECLARATION

A dividend may only be declared if Tag-n-Ride satisfies the **solvency and liquidity test** (Companies Act s46):

- [ ] **Solvency:** Assets fairly valued must equal or exceed liabilities after the dividend
- [ ] **Liquidity:** Company will be able to pay its debts as they become due in the ordinary course of business for the next **12 months** after the dividend
- [ ] A special or ordinary resolution of the Board approving the dividend (see Board Resolution template)
- [ ] Dividend tax withheld and paid to SARS within **15 business days** of payment date

## DIVIDENDS TAX RATES

| Recipient | Rate |
|-----------|------|
| South African natural person shareholder | **20%** |
| SA company holding ≥ 10% (inter-company exemption) | **0%** (exempt — provide STC credit / declaration) |
| Approved public benefit organisation | 0% |
| Foreign shareholder (subject to tax treaty) | Reduced rate per treaty (e.g., UK: 10%, Mauritius: 5%) |
