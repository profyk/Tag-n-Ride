# PAYE / UIF / SDL EMPLOYER REGISTRATION GUIDE
## TAG-N-RIDE (PTY) LTD

**Ref:** TNR-TAX-EMP-001
**Applicable Laws:**
- Income Tax Act 58 of 1962 (PAYE)
- Unemployment Insurance Fund Act 63 of 2001 (UIF)
- Skills Development Levies Act 9 of 1999 (SDL)

---

## OVERVIEW

Tag-n-Ride, as an employer, is required to register for and administer three separate employer obligations:

| Tax | Collected By | Rate | Due |
|-----|-------------|------|-----|
| **PAYE** (Pay As You Earn) | SARS | Per tax tables (0%–45%) | 7th of following month |
| **UIF** | SARS / UIF | 1% employee + 1% employer = 2% | 7th of following month |
| **SDL** | SARS | 1% of total leviable amount | 7th of following month |

**SDL exemption:** Companies with total annual payroll **below R500,000** are exempt from SDL.

---

## STEP 1 — PAYE REGISTRATION (EMP101)

### Documents Required
- [ ] CIPC registration certificate (COR14.3 or CK1)
- [ ] Director's certified ID
- [ ] Company income tax reference number
- [ ] Proof of business address
- [ ] Bank account details (for refunds)

### Process
1. Log in to SARS eFiling
2. Select **Register > PAYE Employer**
3. Complete **EMP101** form
4. SARS issues PAYE reference number (10-digit, starts with 7)
5. Use this reference on all EMP201 monthly submissions

---

## STEP 2 — UIF REGISTRATION

UIF registration happens automatically when you register for PAYE.
- SARS notifies the Department of Employment and Labour
- Alternatively, register via **uFiling** (www.ufiling.co.za)

### UIF Obligations
- Deduct 1% from each employee's remuneration
- Contribute additional 1% as employer
- Maximum salary for UIF calculation: **R17,712/month** (R212,544/year) — adjust for annual CPI

**Excluded from UIF:**
- Independent contractors
- Directors earning only director's fees (not salary)
- Employees working fewer than 24 hours per month

---

## STEP 3 — SDL REGISTRATION

SDL is registered simultaneously with PAYE via EMP101.

### SDL Calculation
```
SDL = 1% × (Total Remuneration - Exempt amounts)
```

**Exempt from SDL:**
- Remuneration of employees earning less than R2,000/month
- Employees over 65 receiving old-age benefits
- Public benefit organizations

### SETA Selection
Tag-n-Ride falls under **MICTS SETA** (Media, Information and Communication Technologies Sector Education and Training Authority).
- SETA reg number to be obtained upon first payment
- Skills development levies fund Workplace Skills Plans (WSP) and Annual Training Reports (ATR)
- Company can claim back up to 20% of SDL paid via mandatory grants (WSP/ATR submitted by 30 April each year)

## MONTHLY PAYROLL CYCLE

| Date | Action |
|------|--------|
| Last day of month | Run payroll, calculate PAYE/UIF/SDL |
| 1st–7th of following month | Submit EMP201 return on eFiling |
| 7th of following month | Pay PAYE + UIF + SDL to SARS |
| 28 Feb each year | Reconcile and submit EMP501 (bi-annual) |
| 31 May each year | Submit final EMP501 reconciliation + IRP5/IT3(a) |

---

## EMPLOYEE TAX CERTIFICATES

- **IRP5** — issued to employees who had tax deducted
- **IT3(a)** — issued to employees with zero tax deducted (e.g., below threshold)
- Must be issued by **31 May** each year
- Must be submitted to SARS electronically

---

## PENALTIES & INTEREST

| Offence | Penalty |
|---------|---------|
| Late EMP201 submission | R100/month per return (admin penalty) |
| Non-payment of PAYE | 10% of amount plus interest at prescribed rate |
| Under-deduction from employees | Company liable — not employee |
| Failure to register | Up to R10,000 penalty |
