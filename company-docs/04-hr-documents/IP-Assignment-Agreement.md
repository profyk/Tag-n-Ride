## 1. BACKGROUND

1.1 The Assignor has been / will be engaged by the Company as **[employee / contractor / advisor / co-founder]** in connection with the development of the Tag-n-Ride QR-based ride payment platform and related technology.

1.2 In the course of this engagement, the Assignor has created or will create certain Intellectual Property ("IP") for, on behalf of, or in connection with the Company's Business.

1.3 The Parties wish to ensure that all such IP is clearly vested in and owned by the Company.

---

## 2. ASSIGNMENT

### 2.1 Present Assignment

The Assignor hereby irrevocably assigns, transfers, and conveys to the Company all right, title, and interest in and to all IP created by the Assignor in connection with the Company's Business, whether created before or after the date of this Agreement ("**Assigned IP**"), including:

  (a) All **source code**, scripts, databases, APIs, algorithms, software, and applications;
  (b) All **user interface designs**, wireframes, prototypes, and design assets;
  (c) All **trade marks**, logos, brand names, and identifiers (including "Tag-n-Ride" and all derivatives);
  (d) All **inventions**, innovations, improvements, and processes;
  (e) All **technical documentation**, specifications, manuals, and training materials;
  (f) All **data models**, database schemas, and data architectures;
  (g) All **business methods** and operational processes developed for the Company;
  (h) All **domain names**, social media handles, app store accounts, and digital assets;
  (i) All **marketing materials**, creative content, and copywriting;
  (j) All other works of **authorship** protected by copyright or similar rights.

### 2.2 Future Assignment

The Assignor agrees that any IP created in the future in connection with the Business is automatically assigned to the Company upon creation, without any further act required.

## 3. MORAL RIGHTS

3.1 To the maximum extent permitted by law, the Assignor waives all moral rights (including rights of attribution and integrity) in the Assigned IP.

3.2 The Company may use, modify, adapt, and commercialise the Assigned IP without attribution to the Assignor, at its sole discretion.

## 4. FURTHER ASSURANCES

4.1 The Assignor shall, at the Company's request and expense:
  (a) Execute any additional documents, assignments, or registrations required to perfect the Company's ownership of the Assigned IP in any jurisdiction;
  (b) Assist in any patent, trademark, or copyright registration processes;
  (c) Provide all reasonably necessary information and assistance to protect the Company's IP rights.

4.2 If the Assignor is unable or unavailable to execute such documents, the Assignor hereby appoints the Company as their attorney-in-fact for this limited purpose.
