## 1. PURPOSE

---

## 2. DEFINITION OF CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION

2.1 "**Confidential Information**" means all information disclosed by Tag-n-Ride to the Receiving Party (whether in writing, orally, digitally, or by any other means) that is:
  (a) Marked as "Confidential" or "Proprietary"; or
  (b) Of a nature that a reasonable person would understand to be confidential.

2.2 Confidential Information includes, without limitation:
  (a) Source code, algorithms, APIs, database structures, and technical architecture;
  (b) Business plans, financial projections, revenue data, and pricing strategies;
  (c) Customer and user data, driver and fleet owner information;
  (d) Investor information and cap table details;
  (e) Partnership and supplier agreements;
  (f) Product roadmaps and unreleased features;
  (g) Marketing strategies, brand assets, and competitive intelligence;
  (h) Operational processes and internal procedures;
  (i) Any information relating to the Company's payment processing systems and Stitch integration;
  (j) Any other information that gives the Company a competitive advantage.

2.3 Confidential Information **excludes** information that:
  (a) Is already in the public domain (other than through a breach of this Agreement);
  (b) Was already known to the Receiving Party prior to disclosure and without breach of any obligation;
  (c) Is independently developed by the Receiving Party without reference to the Confidential Information;
  (d) Is required to be disclosed by law or court order (provided that the Receiving Party gives Tag-n-Ride prompt written notice to seek a protective order).

---

## 3. OBLIGATIONS OF THE RECEIVING PARTY

3.1 The Receiving Party agrees to:
  (a) Keep all Confidential Information strictly confidential;
  (b) Use Confidential Information solely for the Purpose;
  (c) Not disclose Confidential Information to any third party without the prior written consent of Tag-n-Ride;
  (d) Protect Confidential Information with at least the same level of care as the Receiving Party applies to its own confidential information, but in no case less than **reasonable care**;
  (e) Limit access to Confidential Information to those of the Receiving Party's employees or advisors who have a genuine need-to-know for the Purpose and who are bound by equivalent confidentiality obligations;
  (f) Promptly notify Tag-n-Ride upon becoming aware of any unauthorised disclosure or use of Confidential Information.

---

## 4. PERMITTED DISCLOSURES

4.1 The Receiving Party may disclose Confidential Information to:
  (a) Its legal counsel, auditors, and professional advisors, who are bound by professional confidentiality obligations;
  (b) Employees who require the information solely for the Purpose, provided each such person is informed of the confidential nature.

4.2 The Receiving Party remains responsible for any breach by its employees, advisors, or agents.

---

## 5. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

5.1 Nothing in this Agreement transfers or licenses any Intellectual Property rights. All Confidential Information remains the exclusive property of Tag-n-Ride.

5.2 No licence to use, copy, or replicate Tag-n-Ride's technology, branding, or proprietary systems is granted by this Agreement.

---

## 6. RETURN OR DESTRUCTION OF INFORMATION

6.1 Upon written request by Tag-n-Ride, or upon termination of the Purpose or this Agreement, the Receiving Party shall:
  (a) Return all documents, materials, and electronic files containing Confidential Information; and
  (b) Permanently delete all copies of Confidential Information from electronic systems.

6.2 The Receiving Party shall confirm in writing within **5 (five) business days** that such return or destruction has been completed.

---

## 7. TERM AND DURATION

7.1 This Agreement shall commence on the date signed and shall remain in force for **2 (two) years** from the date of execution, unless terminated earlier by mutual written agreement.

7.2 The confidentiality obligations in Clauses 3, 5, and 6 shall survive the expiry or termination of this Agreement for a further period of **5 (five) years**.

---

## 8. REMEDIES

8.1 The Receiving Party acknowledges that a breach of this Agreement would cause irreparable harm to Tag-n-Ride that cannot be adequately compensated by monetary damages alone.

8.2 In addition to any other remedy available at law, Tag-n-Ride shall be entitled to seek immediate injunctive or other equitable relief from a competent court without the need to post bond or security.

8.3 Nothing in this clause limits Tag-n-Ride's right to claim damages.
