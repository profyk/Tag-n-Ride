## 3. LIMITATIONS

The Agent may NOT without express written approval of the CEO or Board:
- Incur debt or sign loan agreements on behalf of the Company
- Sell, encumber, or alienate Company assets
- Make payments exceeding **R[AMOUNT]** per transaction
- Grant sub-powers of attorney
- Bind the Company to multi-year contracts
- Make representations to investors or the media
