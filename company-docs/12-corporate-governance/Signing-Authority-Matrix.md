## PURPOSE

This matrix defines who is authorised to approve and sign various types of documents and transactions on behalf of Tag-n-Ride (Pty) Ltd. Unauthorised commitments are void and may result in personal liability.

## GENERAL PRINCIPLES

1. **No individual may authorise payment to themselves** — must be approved by another T2/T1 signatory.
2. **Contract review before signature:** All contracts with a value or risk above R50,000 must be reviewed by legal counsel before CEO signature.
3. **Budget alignment:** Commitments must be within approved budget lines — out-of-budget items escalate one level.
4. **Records:** Signed originals must be filed in the relevant company-docs folder and a register entry made.
5. **Delegation:** A signatory may not sub-delegate authority to a lower tier without CEO/Board approval.
