## 3. EQUITY AND VESTING

### 3.1 Co-Founder Equity

3.1.1 In consideration for the Co-Founder's contributions and commitments, the Company shall issue the Co-Founder **[NUMBER]** Shares representing **[X%]** of the fully diluted share capital of the Company (the "Co-Founder Shares").

3.1.2 The Co-Founder Shares are subject to the vesting schedule below. Unvested shares may be repurchased by the Company at nominal value upon a Co-Founder's departure.

### 3.2 Vesting Schedule

| Period | Shares Vesting | Cumulative % |
|--------|---------------|--------------|
| Month 0–12 (Cliff) | 0 | 0% |
| Month 12 (Cliff unlock) | 25% of total | 25% |
| Months 13–48 (monthly) | 1/48th per month | Up to 100% |

*Total vesting period: 4 years with a 1-year cliff.*

### 3.3 Acceleration

3.3.1 **Single Trigger:** Upon an Exit Event (trade sale, merger, IPO), all unvested Co-Founder Shares shall vest in full.

3.3.2 **Good Leaver:** Upon a Good Leaver event, all unvested shares vest immediately.

3.3.3 **Bad Leaver:** Upon a Bad Leaver event:
  (a) Only vested shares are retained;
  (b) The Company shall have the right to repurchase vested shares at fair market value, exercisable within **60 (sixty) days** of departure;
  (c) All unvested shares are forfeited immediately.

### 3.4 Issuance

Shares will be issued in the Company's share register upon execution of this Agreement, subject to vesting. Share certificates for unvested shares shall be held in escrow by the Company until such time as they vest.

### 4.1 Salary

4.1.1 The Co-Founder shall receive a monthly salary of **R[AMOUNT]** per month, reviewed annually by the Board.

## 5. FOUNDER DECISION-MAKING AND CONTROL

### 5.1 CEO Authority

5.1.1 Mr Profy T D Keakile, as Founder and CEO, shall retain **final decision-making authority** on all matters related to the Company's strategy, technology, product, and operations.

5.1.2 The Co-Founder shall operate within the authority delegated to them by the CEO and shall not commit the Company to any obligation without the CEO's prior written approval, except for routine operational matters within their designated role.

### 5.2 Voting

5.2.1 Each Share carries one vote. The Founder's shareholding shall at all times represent a **controlling interest** until such time as agreed otherwise in an amended Shareholders Agreement.

5.2.2 The Parties agree to vote their Shares to implement the provisions of this Agreement and the Company's MOI.

---

## 6. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

6.1 All work product, inventions, software, designs, and any other IP created by the Co-Founder in connection with the Company's Business — whether before or during the term of this Agreement — shall be the exclusive property of the Company.

6.2 The Co-Founder hereby irrevocably assigns and transfers to the Company all IP created in connection with the Business.

6.3 The Co-Founder shall maintain records of all such IP and execute any further documents required to perfect the Company's ownership thereof.

6.4 This IP assignment survives the termination of this Agreement.

---

## 7. CONFIDENTIALITY

7.1 The Co-Founder agrees to keep strictly confidential all trade secrets, business strategies, financial information, client data, technical specifications, and any other proprietary information of the Company.

7.2 Confidential information shall not be disclosed to any third party without the prior written consent of the CEO, except as required by law.

7.3 This confidentiality obligation shall survive termination of this Agreement for **5 (five) years**.

---

## 8. NON-COMPETE AND NON-SOLICITATION

### 8.1 Non-Compete

During the term of this Agreement and for **24 (twenty-four) months** thereafter, the Co-Founder shall not:
  (a) Directly or indirectly engage in, or assist any entity engaged in, a business that competes with the Company's Business in any market where the Company operates or plans to operate;
  (b) Use the Company's IP, trade secrets, or confidential information for any competing purpose.

### 8.2 Non-Solicitation

During the term of this Agreement and for **24 (twenty-four) months** thereafter, the Co-Founder shall not:
  (a) Solicit, induce, or assist any other person to solicit any employee, contractor, or advisor of the Company to leave;
  (b) Solicit any client, customer, driver, operator, or partner of the Company for a competing business;
  (c) Disparage the Company, its products, or its founders publicly.

---

## 9. DEPARTURE AND LEAVER PROVISIONS

### 9.1 Voluntary Resignation (Bad Leaver)

9.1.1 The Co-Founder shall give **3 (three) months** written notice of resignation.

9.1.2 Upon resignation, only vested shares are retained. The Company may exercise its repurchase right within **60 days** at fair market value.

### 9.2 Termination for Cause (Bad Leaver)

9.2.1 The Company may terminate the Co-Founder for cause including, without limitation:
  (a) Material breach of this Agreement not remedied within **10 business days** of written notice;
  (b) Gross misconduct or dishonesty;
  (c) Breach of fiduciary duty;
  (d) Conviction of a criminal offence.

9.2.2 Upon termination for cause, unvested shares are forfeited. The Company may exercise its repurchase right on vested shares at **par value** (not fair market value).

### 9.3 Good Leaver Events

9.3.1 Upon death or permanent disability, all shares vest immediately and pass to the Co-Founder's estate or nominated beneficiary.
