## 1. INVESTMENT

1.1 The Investor agrees to invest **R[AMOUNT]** ("**the Investment Amount**") in the Company in exchange for the right to receive Shares upon the occurrence of a **Conversion Event**, as described in Clause 3.

1.2 The Investment Amount shall be transferred to the Company's bank account within **5 (five) business days** of execution.

1.3 This SAFE is **not a loan** and carries no interest or repayment obligation, except as provided in Clause 5 (Dissolution).

---

## 2. CONVERSION TERMS

### 2.1 Conversion Discount

Upon a Conversion Event, the Investment Amount shall convert into Shares at a **[20%] discount** to the price per share paid by new investors in that round ("**Discount Rate**").

*Example: If Series A shares are priced at R10.00 per share, SAFE converts at R8.00 per share.*

### 2.2 Valuation Cap

Alternatively, the Investment Amount shall convert at whichever price is **lower** of:
  (a) The Discounted Price (Clause 2.1); or
  (b) The Valuation Cap Price = **R[VALUATION CAP AMOUNT]** divided by the Company's fully diluted share count immediately prior to the Conversion Event.

*The Valuation Cap protects the Investor from excessive dilution if the Company raises at a very high valuation.*

### 2.3 Conversion Formula

**Shares Issued = Investment Amount ÷ Conversion Price**

Where Conversion Price = Lower of (Discounted Price, Valuation Cap Price).

---

## 3. CONVERSION EVENTS

### 3.1 Equity Financing (Automatic Conversion)

Upon a **Qualified Financing** (a priced equity round raising **R[MINIMUM AMOUNT]** or more), this SAFE shall automatically convert into the same class of shares issued to new investors in that round, at the Conversion Price.

### 3.2 Exit Event (Optional Conversion)

Upon an Exit Event (trade sale, merger, acquisition, or IPO), the Investor may elect to:
  (a) Convert the SAFE into Shares at the Conversion Price and participate in the exit proceeds; or
  (b) Receive a cash payout equal to **[1x / 1.5x]** the Investment Amount from the exit proceeds, before ordinary shareholders.

### 3.3 Dissolution

If the Company is wound up before a Conversion Event, the Investor shall receive the lesser of:
  (a) The Investment Amount; or
  (b) Their pro-rata share of the Company's assets after satisfying creditors (but before ordinary shareholders).

---

## 4. INVESTOR RIGHTS PRIOR TO CONVERSION

4.1 The Investor shall have the right to receive:
  (a) Quarterly financial updates;
  (b) Notice of any material adverse events affecting the Company;
  (c) Access to Company records for due diligence purposes, with reasonable notice.

4.2 The Investor shall not have voting rights prior to conversion.

4.3 The Company shall notify the Investor in writing within **5 (five) business days** of any Conversion Event trigger.

---

## 5. MOST FAVOURED NATION (MFN) CLAUSE

5.1 If the Company issues any subsequent SAFEs to other investors on terms more favourable than this SAFE, the Investor shall have the option to amend this SAFE to incorporate those more favourable terms.

5.2 The Company shall notify the Investor within **10 business days** of issuing any subsequent SAFE.

---

## 6. COMPANY REPRESENTATIONS

The Company represents and warrants that:

  (a) It is duly incorporated in South Africa and in good standing with CIPC;
  (b) The execution of this SAFE has been duly authorised by the Board;
  (c) There are no material undisclosed liabilities or litigation;
  (d) The Company owns or has valid licences to all Intellectual Property used in its business;
  (e) The Investment Amount will be used for legitimate business purposes as described in Clause 7.

## 7. USE OF PROCEEDS

The Investment Amount shall be used for:
  (a) Product development and engineering;
  (b) Marketing, growth, and user acquisition;
  (c) Regulatory compliance;
  (d) Operational costs and working capital.

The Company shall not use the Investment Amount for personal expenses, related-party payments, or purposes unrelated to the Business without the Investor's written consent.
