### 4. INVESTOR RIGHTS

#### 4.1 Board Representation
- Investor shall have the right to appoint **one (1) non-executive director** to the Board.
- If a future round brings the Investor's stake below **10%**, the board seat right lapses.

#### 4.2 Information Rights
The Investor shall receive:
  (a) Monthly management accounts within **15 days** of month-end;
  (b) Audited annual financial statements within **90 days** of financial year-end;
  (c) Quarterly investor update reports including key metrics (GMV, active users, transaction volume);
  (d) Advance notice of all Board meetings and copies of board packs.

#### 4.3 Observer Rights
If board seat rights lapse, Investor retains the right to attend Board meetings as an observer (non-voting).

#### 4.4 Anti-Dilution Protection
- The Investor shall have **weighted average anti-dilution** protection in any future down round.
- This protection does not apply to ESOP issuances or shares issued in an up-round.

#### 4.5 Pre-Emptive Rights
The Investor shall have pro-rata pre-emptive rights to participate in future funding rounds to maintain their percentage ownership, exercisable within **15 business days** of written notice.

#### 4.6 Liquidation Preference
In the event of a winding up or sale of the Company:
  (a) **1x non-participating liquidation preference** — Investor receives their investment back before ordinary shareholders receive anything.
  (b) After the liquidation preference, remaining proceeds are distributed to ordinary shareholders only.

### 5. FOUNDER PROTECTIONS

#### 5.1 Founder Vesting
Founder shares remain subject to a **4-year reverse vesting schedule** (25% cliff at year 1, monthly thereafter).

#### 5.2 Founder Control
Mr Profy T D Keakile shall retain operational control as CEO until:
  (a) An Exit Event; or
  (b) A vote by shareholders holding more than 75% to replace the CEO.

#### 5.3 Protective Provisions for Founder
The following actions require Founder consent in addition to Board approval:
  (a) Any change to the Company's primary business;
  (b) Issuance of shares that would dilute the Founder below **25%** without the Founder's written consent;
  (c) Any amendment to this Term Sheet or the Investment Agreement that adversely affects the Founder.

---

### 6. INVESTOR PROTECTIVE PROVISIONS

The following actions shall require Investor approval (in addition to Board approval) while the Investor holds **10% or more** of Shares:

  (a) Changes to the Company's MOI that adversely affect investor rights;
  (b) Any transaction with a related party exceeding **R100,000**;
  (c) Dividend declarations before an Exit Event;
  (d) Incurring debt exceeding **R500,000** in aggregate;
  (e) Any sale of material Company assets outside the ordinary course of business;
  (f) Changes to the ESOP Pool size.

---

### 7. EXIT AND LIQUIDITY

#### 7.1 Drag-Along
If shareholders holding **75% or more** accept an Exit offer, they may drag all remaining shareholders.

#### 7.2 Tag-Along
Investor has tag-along rights on any transfer of **25% or more** of Shares by the Founder.

#### 7.3 IPO
Investor and Founder agree to cooperate in good faith towards an IPO if market conditions and company performance support it.

#### 7.4 Redemption Right
If no Exit Event occurs within **7 (seven) years** of this investment, the Investor shall have the right to require the Company to buy back their shares at fair market value, subject to the Company's solvency and liquidity constraints.
