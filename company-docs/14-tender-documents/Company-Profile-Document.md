### 1.2 Executive Summary

Tag-n-Ride (Pty) Ltd is a South African financial technology company specialising in digital payment solutions for the public transport sector, with a primary focus on the minibus taxi industry. The company's flagship Platform enables passengers to pay taxi fares cashlessly using QR code technology and mobile wallet integration, directly addressing South Africa's urgent need for financial inclusion in the informal transport sector.

Tag-n-Ride was founded with the vision of bridging the digital divide in South Africa's public transport network — a sector that accounts for an estimated 69% of all commuter trips in the country, yet remains almost entirely cash-dependent. By enabling digital payments, Tag-n-Ride delivers benefits across the entire transport value chain: passengers gain safety and convenience; operators gain financial transparency and faster access to funds; associations gain credible data for route management; and government gains visibility into an informal sector that has historically been difficult to monitor.

The company is majority black-owned and proudly South African, with operations designed to create employment, develop driver and operator financial literacy, and contribute to the National Development Plan's goals for inclusive economic growth and digital transformation.

## 2. TECHNOLOGY PLATFORM

### 2.1 Platform Description

The Tag-n-Ride Platform is a mobile-first, cloud-hosted digital payment system comprising the following components:

| Component | Description |
|---|---|
| **Passenger App** | Mobile application enabling passengers to pay fares, view trip history, and manage their payment methods |
| **Driver App** | Application for registered drivers to track daily transactions, confirm payments, and view shift summaries |
| **Operator Dashboard** | Web and mobile dashboard for taxi operators and fleet owners to manage vehicles, drivers, wallets, and reports |
| **QR Code Devices** | Vehicle-specific QR codes (physical sticker and/or digital screen) enabling contactless payments |
| **Digital Wallet System** | Electronic wallets for operators, enabling settlement of fare proceeds net of platform fees |
| **Association Portal** | Read-only analytics portal for taxi associations to monitor aggregated route performance data |
| **Admin Console** | Internal management system for KYC, compliance, reporting, and support |
