## PART C: DECLARATION OF INDEPENDENT PRICE DETERMINATION

### C.1 Main Declaration

I, Mr Profy T D Keakile, Chief Executive Officer of Tag-n-Ride (Pty) Ltd, do hereby declare that the price/s, rates, and/or terms contained in this bid/tender submission are independently determined and represent Tag-n-Ride's genuine commercial offering, and that:

**C.1.1 No collusion:**

The bid price was not arrived at through any understanding, arrangement, agreement, or consultation (express or implied) with any competitor, other bidder, supplier, service provider, or any other person, regarding:

(a) Prices, pricing formulas, pricing methodologies, or pricing strategies;

(b) Profit margins, discounts, or concessions;

(c) The decision to submit or not submit a bid;

(d) The allocation of contracts between bidders.

**C.1.2 No disclosure:**

The contents of this bid submission, including all pricing, have not been communicated to any competitor or other person, other than the procuring authority, prior to the official opening of bids.

**C.1.3 No solicitation of improper advantage:**

Tag-n-Ride has not, directly or indirectly, offered, promised, granted, or agreed to grant any person any consideration — whether in the form of money, gifts, hospitality, employment, or any other advantage — in order to obtain this contract or to gain an unfair advantage in the bidding process.

**C.1.4 No bid rigging:**

Tag-n-Ride has not entered into any agreement, arrangement, or understanding with any other bidder, whether for the purpose of:

(a) One party withdrawing from the tender process;

(b) Submitting a deliberately inflated or deflated bid;

(c) Agreeing who will win the contract;

(d) Dividing contracts between competitors after award.

Such conduct constitutes bid rigging, which is a per se prohibited practice under section 4(1)(b)(iii) of the Competition Act 89 of 1998 and may also constitute corruption under section 12 of PRECCA.

## PART D: ANTI-CORRUPTION PLEDGE

### D.1 Corruption Prohibition

I confirm that Tag-n-Ride (Pty) Ltd:

D.1.1 Has not offered, promised, or given any bribe, gift, hospitality, payment, or other advantage to any official, employee, or representative of the procuring authority in connection with this bid;

D.1.2 Has not engaged any person acting as a broker or intermediary who would be compensated based on the outcome of this bid through corrupt means;

D.1.3 Has not colluded with any employee or official of the procuring authority to obtain, or attempt to obtain, preferential consideration of this bid;

D.1.4 Commits to reporting to the South African Police Service (SAPS) or the relevant procurement authority any approach by any person requesting a bribe or improper consideration in connection with this bid.

### D.2 PRECCA Awareness

I acknowledge that:

D.2.1 The Prevention and Combating of Corrupt Activities Act 12 of 2004 (PRECCA) imposes a duty on persons in positions of authority (including directors and senior managers) to report corrupt activities under section 34;

D.2.2 Corruption in procurement is punishable by up to fifteen (15) years imprisonment under PRECCA;

D.2.3 Any contract awarded as a result of corrupt, fraudulent, or collusive conduct may be declared void and the proceeds recovered.
