## PART 6: FRONTING AND MISREPRESENTATION DECLARATION

I hereby declare, on behalf of Tag-n-Ride (Pty) Ltd, that:

6.1 Tag-n-Ride does not engage in BBBEE fronting as defined in section 1 of the B-BBEE Act 53 of 2003;

6.2 No arrangement exists that would give any person who is not black (as defined in the B-BBEE Act) an economic interest or benefit that disproportionately exceeds their BBBEE ownership participation in Tag-n-Ride;

6.3 No transaction, arrangement, or scheme has been entered into that has the effect of misrepresenting Tag-n-Ride's BBBEE status;

6.4 I am aware that:

(a) Fronting constitutes a criminal offence under section 26B of the B-BBEE Act, punishable by a fine or imprisonment for up to ten (10) years, or both;

(b) Misrepresentation of BBBEE status in a tender submission constitutes fraud and may result in disqualification, contract cancellation, and referral to law enforcement;

(c) Relevant information may be reported to the B-BBEE Commission established under the B-BBEE Act.
