## KEY TERMS

1. **Exercising your Options:** Once vested, you may exercise your Options by submitting a written Exercise Notice to the Company accompanied by payment of the Exercise Price. Your Options will then be converted to ordinary shares.

2. **Leaver Provisions:**
   - *Good Leaver* (death, disability, retrenchment, Board-approved departure): All unvested Options vest immediately. You have 90 days to exercise.
   - *Bad Leaver* (resignation, dismissal for cause): Unvested Options are forfeited. Vested Options must be exercised within 30 days.

3. **Exit Event:** Upon an acquisition, merger, or IPO of the Company, all your unvested Options vest immediately.

4. **Non-Transferability:** Options may not be transferred, sold, pledged, or encumbered.

5. **Tax:** You are responsible for all tax obligations arising from your Options. The Company will assist with required PAYE deductions and tax certificates.

6. **Confidentiality:** The details of this grant are confidential and may not be disclosed to other employees or third parties.
