## TAG-N-RIDE (PTY) LTD

**Version:** 1.0
**Effective Date:** [DATE]
**Owner:** CEO — Mr Profy T D Keakile

---

## 1. PURPOSE

This Policy governs all marketing, advertising, and communications activities of Tag-n-Ride (Pty) Ltd to ensure they are:
- Accurate, honest, and compliant with South African law;
- Consistent with the Tag-n-Ride brand;
- Protective of users' trust and the Company's reputation;
- Compliant with the Consumer Protection Act, POPIA, and ECT Act.

---

## 2. AUTHORISATION

### 2.1 Who Can Approve Marketing

| Type of Communication | Approval Required |
|-----------------------|------------------|
| Social media posts (organic) | Marketing Lead / CEO |
| Paid advertising campaigns | CEO |
| Press releases | CEO |
| Influencer partnerships | CEO |
| In-app messaging | Product Lead + CEO |
| Investor or B2B communications | CEO |
| Community / partner co-branding | CEO |

### 2.2 No Unauthorised Publishing

No employee, contractor, or agency may publish, distribute, or broadcast any material in Tag-n-Ride's name without the approval of the relevant authority above.

---

## 3. ADVERTISING STANDARDS

### 3.1 Truth and Accuracy

All marketing materials must:
  (a) Be truthful, accurate, and not misleading;
  (b) Not make unsubstantiated claims about platform capabilities, fees, or user benefits;
  (c) Not compare competitors in a disparaging or inaccurate way;
  (d) Clearly disclose all fees and conditions associated with advertised offers.

### 3.2 Consumer Protection Act (CPA) Compliance

All advertising must comply with the **Consumer Protection Act 68 of 2008**, including:
  (a) No bait advertising — if a promotional offer is made, it must be available;
  (b) Clear disclosure of terms and conditions for any promotions or bonuses;
  (c) No false labelling or description of services.

### 3.3 Financial Services Advertising

As a fintech platform:
  (a) We do not make guarantees of returns or earnings;
  (b) All claims about transaction security or speed must be accurate;
  (c) We do not advertise investment products or interest-bearing instruments without appropriate licensing.

---

## 4. DIGITAL MARKETING

### 4.1 Social Media

  (a) All official social media accounts must be managed through approved tools.
  (b) Content calendar must be approved monthly by the CEO or Marketing Lead.
  (c) Community management (replies, comments) must be handled by authorised staff only.
  (d) Negative or complaint comments must be addressed promptly and professionally — never ignored or deleted without a response.

### 4.2 Email and SMS Marketing

  (a) Marketing emails and SMS messages may only be sent to users who have **opted in** to receive them (POPIA/ECTA requirement).
  (b) Every marketing communication must include a clear, functioning **opt-out mechanism**.
  (c) Opt-out requests must be processed within **5 business days**.
  (d) Marketing lists must be cleaned of opt-outs and invalid contacts monthly.

### 4.3 Paid Advertising

  (a) All paid ads (Google, Meta, TikTok, etc.) must be approved by the CEO before going live.
  (b) Ad copy must comply with the platform's advertising policies and South African law.
  (c) Performance metrics must be reported to the CEO monthly.

---

## 5. INFLUENCER AND PARTNERSHIP MARKETING

### 5.1 Influencer Agreements

  (a) All influencer engagements must be documented with a signed **Influencer/Partnership Agreement**.
  (b) Influencers must clearly disclose that their content is paid/sponsored (in compliance with ASA guidelines).
  (c) Influencers may not make claims about Tag-n-Ride beyond the approved messaging brief.
  (d) Influencer content must be approved by Tag-n-Ride before publication.

### 5.2 Partnership Co-Marketing

  (a) Any co-branded marketing with a third party requires a signed partnership agreement.
  (b) Partners may not use the Tag-n-Ride logo or brand without written permission.

---

## 6. CONTENT STANDARDS

### 6.1 Prohibited Content

Tag-n-Ride marketing shall not contain:
  (a) Discriminatory, offensive, or culturally insensitive language or imagery;
  (b) Claims that violate any applicable South African law or regulation;
  (c) Misleading pricing or fee information;
  (d) Content targeting minors;
  (e) Explicit, violent, or inappropriate content.

### 6.2 Cultural Sensitivity

Given that Tag-n-Ride serves diverse South African communities, all marketing must be:
  (a) Inclusive and representative of our user base;
  (b) Respectful of cultural and linguistic diversity;
  (c) Reviewed for cultural appropriateness before publishing.

---

## 7. CRISIS COMMUNICATIONS

7.1 In the event of a public relations crisis (negative press, viral complaint, service outage), the following protocol applies:

  (a) Do not respond publicly without CEO authorisation;
  (b) The CEO is the sole authorised spokesperson for crisis communications;
  (c) All public statements must be factually accurate and approved before release;
  (d) Monitor all channels and escalate to the CEO within **1 hour** of awareness.

---

## 8. MARKETING BUDGET AND ROI

8.1 Marketing spend must be approved as part of the annual budget or by special CEO approval.

8.2 All marketing campaigns must include defined objectives and KPIs.

8.3 Monthly marketing performance reports shall include:
  (a) Spend vs budget;
  (b) Key metrics: impressions, clicks, installs, activations, cost per acquisition (CPA);
  (c) Attribution analysis.
