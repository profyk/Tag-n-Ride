## 1. BRAND IDENTITY

### 1.1 Our Brand Promise

Tag-n-Ride exists to make South African taxi payments **simple, safe, and instant**. Our brand should feel:

- **Trustworthy** — people trust us with their money; confidence and reliability must show
- **Accessible** — built for every South African, not just the tech-savvy
- **Modern** — fintech innovation for a traditional industry
- **Local** — proudly South African, connected to township and urban life

### 1.2 Brand Personality

| Trait | In practice |
|-------|------------|
| **Confident** | Direct, clear language. No corporate jargon. |
| **Warm** | We speak to users like people, not account numbers. |
| **Reliable** | Consistent, predictable, and honest about issues. |
| **Progressive** | Always improving, forward-thinking. |

### 2.1 Company Name

- Correct: **Tag-n-Ride** (hyphenated, capitalised T and R)
- Incorrect: tag n ride, Tag N Ride, TagnRide, TAGNRIDE

### 2.2 Logo Usage Rules

- The Tag-n-Ride logo must **never** be stretched, distorted, or recoloured.
- Always use the provided logo files — do not recreate the logo in text.
- Maintain a clear exclusion zone (minimum 16px / 5mm) around the logo.
- Do not place the logo on backgrounds that reduce visibility.
- Approved logo versions: Full colour, White (for dark backgrounds), Black (for print).

### 2.3 Trademark Notice

All external uses of the Tag-n-Ride name and logo must include: **™** (pending registration) or **®** (upon successful CIPC trademark registration).

## 5. TONE OF VOICE

### 5.1 Writing Principles

**Be direct.** Say what you mean in as few words as possible.
> ✅ "Your payment is confirmed."
> ❌ "We are pleased to inform you that your payment has been successfully processed."

**Be human.** Write for a real person, not a committee.
> ✅ "Something went wrong — try again."
> ❌ "An unexpected error has occurred. Please retry the transaction."

**Be transparent.** If something failed, say so honestly.
> ✅ "Your withdrawal is taking longer than usual. We're looking into it."
> ❌ "Please be advised that your withdrawal is currently being processed."

**Respect intelligence.** Users are smart — explain why when needed, but don't over-explain.

### 5.2 Language

- Primary app language: **English**
- SMS notifications and key alerts should also be available in **Zulu, Sotho, Xhosa** where resources allow.
- Avoid slang or colloquialisms that may be misunderstood across cultures.

## 6. PHOTOGRAPHY AND IMAGERY

### 6.1 Photographic Style

All imagery used by Tag-n-Ride should:
  (a) Feature real, diverse South African people — commuters, taxi drivers, entrepreneurs;
  (b) Reflect authentic township and urban South African environments;
  (c) Be bright, high-contrast, and optimistic in tone;
  (d) Avoid stereotypes or tokenism.

### 6.2 What to Avoid

- Generic stock photos of generic phones or international cities;
- Low-quality or heavily filtered images;
- Imagery that is culturally insensitive or misrepresentative.

## 8. APPROVED USES AND PROHIBITED USES

### Approved

- Marketing materials created by Tag-n-Ride staff or approved agencies
- Press mentions that accurately describe the Company
- Partner co-branding (requires separate written approval from CEO)

### Prohibited

- Unauthorised use of the logo by third parties
- Creating unofficial Tag-n-Ride merchandise
- Using the brand in a way that implies endorsement without written agreement
- Parody or satirical use without CEO approval
