## RECITALS

A. Tag-n-Ride operates a digital fare payment platform ("**the Platform**") that enables cashless fare payments on minibus taxi routes using QR code technology;

B. The Fleet Owner is a registered member of [ASSOCIATION NAME] and owns a fleet of [NUMBER] minibus taxi vehicles operating on NLTA-registered routes;

C. The Fleet Owner wishes to enrol all or part of their fleet on the Platform and to manage digital fare collection, cashup, and wallet operations for the fleet through a single Fleet Owner account;

D. Tag-n-Ride agrees to provide fleet-specific Platform features, including per-vehicle QR Code Devices, a Fleet Owner master wallet, and driver management tools, subject to the terms of this Agreement.

**NOW THEREFORE** the Parties agree as follows:

---

## PART 1: DEFINITIONS

**"Cashup"** means the process by which daily or periodic fare receipts collected via the Platform for each Registered Vehicle are aggregated and credited to the Fleet Owner Wallet, after deduction of the Platform Fee.

**"Driver Account"** means a Platform account created for a specific Driver assigned to a Registered Vehicle.

**"Driver"** means any person engaged by the Fleet Owner (as an employee, independent contractor, or otherwise) to operate a Registered Vehicle.

**"Fleet Owner Wallet"** means the master Digital Wallet maintained on the Platform in the Fleet Owner's name, consolidating settled receipts from all Registered Vehicles.

**"NLTA Operating Licence"** means the operating licence issued under section 47 of the National Land Transport Act 5 of 2009 for a specific vehicle and route.

**"Platform Fee"** means Tag-n-Ride's service fee per transaction, as set out in Schedule A.

**"QR Code Device"** means the physical or digital QR code assigned to each Registered Vehicle for passenger payments.

**"Registered Vehicle"** means each vehicle enrolled on the Platform by the Fleet Owner, as listed in Schedule B.

## PART 2: FLEET ENROLMENT

### 2.1 Enrolment Process

2.1.1 The Fleet Owner shall submit the documentation listed in Schedule C for each vehicle to be enrolled on the Platform.

2.1.2 Tag-n-Ride shall complete KYC verification of the Fleet Owner entity within ten (10) business days of receiving all required documentation.

2.1.3 Upon successful KYC, Tag-n-Ride shall activate the Fleet Owner account and issue QR Code Devices for each Registered Vehicle.

### 2.2 Fleet Eligibility

Each vehicle to be enrolled must:

| Requirement | Standard |
|---|---|
| NLTA Operating Licence | Valid, covering the route on which the vehicle operates |
| Vehicle Registration | Current and in fleet owner's name (or financed vehicle with owner's interest noted) |
| Roadworthiness Certificate | Valid |
| Association Membership | Fleet Owner must be member in good standing |
| Insurance | At minimum, statutory third-party insurance |

### 2.3 Adding and Removing Vehicles

2.3.1 The Fleet Owner may add new vehicles to the Platform by submitting an update to Schedule B with the required documentation. Tag-n-Ride shall activate new vehicles within five (5) business days of receiving complete documentation.

2.3.2 The Fleet Owner shall notify Tag-n-Ride immediately if any Registered Vehicle is sold, written off, deregistered, or its NLTA Operating Licence lapses. Tag-n-Ride shall deactivate the vehicle's QR Code Device within 24 hours of receiving such notification.

---

## PART 3: PER-VEHICLE QR CODES

### 3.1 Issuance

Tag-n-Ride shall issue a unique QR Code Device for each Registered Vehicle. Each QR Code Device encodes the vehicle registration number, the Fleet Owner's account identifier, and a unique vehicle token.

### 3.2 Vehicle-Specific Linking

3.2.1 All fare payments processed through a vehicle's QR Code Device are attributed to that specific vehicle and consolidated into the Fleet Owner Wallet as part of the daily Cashup.

3.2.2 Vehicle-level transaction reports shall be available to the Fleet Owner via the Platform dashboard.

### 3.3 Fleet Owner's Obligations

The Fleet Owner shall:

3.3.1 Install and maintain QR Code Devices in all Registered Vehicles in accordance with Tag-n-Ride's installation guidelines;

3.3.2 Ensure no QR Code Device is transferred between vehicles without Tag-n-Ride's written approval;

3.3.3 Report lost, stolen, or damaged QR Code Devices to Tag-n-Ride within 24 hours;

3.3.4 Bear the replacement cost for lost or damaged QR Code Devices beyond the first free replacement per vehicle per year.

---

## PART 4: DRIVER MANAGEMENT

### 4.1 Driver Registration

4.1.1 The Fleet Owner is responsible for registering each Driver on the Platform via the Fleet Owner dashboard.

4.1.2 For each Driver, the Fleet Owner must submit:

- Driver's full name and South African ID number
- Valid Professional Driving Permit ("**PDP**") details
- Driver's cell phone number (for app access)
- Vehicle assigned to the Driver

4.1.3 Tag-n-Ride shall create a Driver Account linked to the specific Registered Vehicle assigned.

### 4.2 Driver App Access

4.2.1 Each Driver shall access the Platform via the Tag-n-Ride Driver App installed on their smartphone.

4.2.2 The Driver App enables Drivers to view the daily transaction summary, receive shift-start/end notifications, and confirm passenger payment receipts.

4.2.3 Drivers shall not have access to the Fleet Owner Wallet or the ability to initiate withdrawals.

### 4.3 Fleet Owner's Responsibility for Driver Conduct

4.3.1 The Fleet Owner is solely responsible for the conduct of Drivers operating Registered Vehicles, including compliance with the Tag-n-Ride Code of Conduct.

4.3.2 Tag-n-Ride's liability for any act or omission of a Driver is expressly excluded. The Fleet Owner shall indemnify Tag-n-Ride against any claim, loss, penalty, or regulatory sanction arising from the conduct of any Driver.

4.3.3 If a Driver is suspended or dismissed, the Fleet Owner must deactivate the Driver Account on the Platform within 24 hours.

### 4.4 Incident Reporting

The Fleet Owner shall report to Tag-n-Ride within 48 hours any incident involving a Driver that may have resulted in:
- Fraudulent or manipulated transactions
- Passenger complaints regarding payment
- Physical tampering with a QR Code Device
- Use of the Platform for non-taxi purposes

---

## PART 5: FLEET OWNER WALLET AND CASHUP MECHANICS

### 5.1 Cashup Process

5.1.1 At the end of each operating day, Tag-n-Ride shall process the daily Cashup for all Registered Vehicles.

5.1.2 The daily Cashup shall:

(a) Aggregate all completed fare transactions per Registered Vehicle;

(b) Deduct the Platform Fee per transaction;

(c) Deduct any Chargebacks or reversals attributable to the day's transactions;

(d) Credit the net amount to the Fleet Owner Wallet.

5.1.3 A per-vehicle daily Cashup Report shall be available on the Fleet Owner dashboard by [TIME] each day.

### 5.2 Fleet Owner Wallet

| Feature | Details |
|---|---|
| Wallet Type | Consolidated master wallet (all vehicles) |
| Sub-account View | Per-vehicle breakdown available |
| Withdrawal Method | EFT to linked bank account |
| Standard Withdrawal Processing | 1–2 business days |
| Instant Withdrawal (if available) | Same day (subject to fees in Schedule A) |
| Minimum Withdrawal | R[AMOUNT] |
| Interest on Balance | None |

### 5.3 Vehicle-Level Reporting

The Fleet Owner shall have access to the following reports per Registered Vehicle:

| Report | Frequency | Available Via |
|---|---|---|
| Daily Transaction Summary | Daily | Dashboard / Email |
| Weekly Cashup Summary | Weekly | Dashboard |
| Monthly Revenue Statement | Monthly | Dashboard / Email |
| Driver Activity Report | On demand | Dashboard |
| Chargeback Report | On demand | Dashboard |

## PART 6: LIABILITY FOR DRIVER CONDUCT

### 6.1 Fleet Owner Liability

6.1.1 The Fleet Owner acknowledges and accepts full liability for:

(a) Fraudulent transactions initiated or facilitated by Drivers;

(b) Misuse of QR Code Devices by Drivers;

(c) Failure by Drivers to honour passenger refund entitlements under the Consumer Protection Act 68 of 2008;

(d) Any NLTA non-compliance by Drivers operating Registered Vehicles.

6.1.2 Tag-n-Ride may deduct from the Fleet Owner Wallet any amounts attributable to Driver fraud or misconduct, including Chargebacks, penalties, and investigation costs.

### 6.2 Indemnity

The Fleet Owner hereby indemnifies, defends, and holds harmless Tag-n-Ride and its officers, directors, employees, and agents from and against any and all claims, losses, damages, penalties, fines, and liabilities arising from:

6.2.1 The acts or omissions of any Driver or other person engaged by the Fleet Owner;

6.2.2 Any breach by the Fleet Owner of this Agreement;

6.2.3 Any violation of Applicable Laws by the Fleet Owner or their Drivers.

### 6.3 Tag-n-Ride Liability

Tag-n-Ride's total aggregate liability to the Fleet Owner under this Agreement shall not exceed the total Platform Fees paid by the Fleet Owner in the three (3) months immediately preceding the event giving rise to the claim.

7.2 The Fleet Owner warrants that they have obtained the necessary consent from each Driver for the collection of that Driver's personal information as part of the onboarding process.

7.3 Both Parties shall comply with POPIA in respect of all personal information processed in connection with this Agreement.

## PART 8: TERMINATION

### 8.1 Termination by Fleet Owner

The Fleet Owner may terminate this Agreement on thirty (30) days' written notice, provided all wallets are cleared and no pending disputes or investigations exist.

### 8.2 Termination by Tag-n-Ride

Tag-n-Ride may terminate immediately if:

8.2.1 The Fleet Owner fails KYC or FICA re-verification;

8.2.2 Fraudulent activity is detected across two (2) or more Registered Vehicles within any three (3)-month period;

8.2.3 The Fleet Owner's NLTA Operating Licences for the majority of Registered Vehicles lapse;

8.2.4 The Fleet Owner becomes insolvent.

### 8.3 Consequences

Upon termination:
- All vehicle QR Codes deactivated immediately
- Fleet Owner Wallet balance settled within 30 days (subject to fraud holds)
- Fleet Owner must return all physical QR Code Devices within 14 days
