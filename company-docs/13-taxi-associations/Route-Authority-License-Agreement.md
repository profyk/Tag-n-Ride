## RECITALS

A. The Route Authority holds or administers operating permits and route authorities under the National Land Transport Act 5 of 2009 ("**NLTA**") in respect of the route(s) described in Schedule A ("**the Route**");

B. Tag-n-Ride has developed and operates a digital payment platform that enables passengers to pay taxi fares electronically using QR code technology and mobile wallet systems;

C. Tag-n-Ride wishes to obtain a licence and permission from the Route Authority to deploy and operate its digital payment infrastructure on vehicles operating on the Route, in accordance with NLTA conditions and with the consent of the Route Authority;

D. The Route Authority is willing to grant such licence, subject to compliance with NLTA route conditions, a route access fee, and the other terms set out in this Agreement.

**NOW THEREFORE** the Parties agree as follows:

---

## PART 1: DEFINITIONS

**"Active Vehicle"** means a vehicle operating on the Route that has processed at least one successful transaction on the Platform in the immediately preceding calendar month.

**"Data Sharing Obligations"** means the Route Authority's entitlement to anonymised transaction and route utilisation data as set out in Part 6.

**"Digital Payment Infrastructure"** means Tag-n-Ride's QR Code Devices, server-side payment processing systems, and Driver/Passenger apps deployed on the Route.

**"NLTA"** means the National Land Transport Act 5 of 2009 and all regulations, notices, and provincial legislation issued thereunder.

**"Operating Permit"** means the permit issued under section 47 of the NLTA authorising the operation of a specific vehicle on the Route, as referenced in Schedule A.

**"Platform"** means the Tag-n-Ride digital fare payment platform.

**"Route"** means the specific public transport route(s) described in Schedule A, including the defined origin, destination, intermediate stops, and associated operating conditions.

**"Route Access Fee"** means the monthly fee payable by Tag-n-Ride to the Route Authority for the licence granted under this Agreement, as set out in Schedule B.

**"Route Operator"** means a taxi operator holding a valid Operating Permit for the Route and registered on the Platform.

**"NLTA Route Conditions"** means all conditions attached to the Operating Permits for the Route, including frequency, loading rank arrangements, and route boundaries.

## PART 2: GRANT OF LICENCE

### 2.1 Licence Grant

Subject to the terms of this Agreement, the Route Authority hereby grants Tag-n-Ride a [non-exclusive / exclusive] licence to:

2.1.1 Deploy Digital Payment Infrastructure on vehicles operating on the Route;

2.1.2 Enable cashless fare payments between passengers and Route Operators through the Platform;

2.1.3 Process, settle, and record all digital fare transactions on the Route;

2.1.4 Use the Route reference in Tag-n-Ride's promotional and reporting materials (subject to clause 2.3).

### 2.2 Conditions of Licence

The licence is granted subject to the following conditions:

2.2.1 Tag-n-Ride shall only activate Platform access for Route Operators holding valid Operating Permits for the Route;

2.2.2 The Platform shall not alter or interfere with the NLTA Operating Permit conditions applicable to the Route;

2.2.3 Tag-n-Ride shall not sub-licence its rights under this Agreement to any third party without the Route Authority's prior written consent;

2.2.4 The licence does not grant Tag-n-Ride any operational authority over the transport service — Tag-n-Ride is a payment facilitator only.

### 2.3 Exclusivity

*[Mark applicable option:]*

- [ ] **Non-Exclusive:** The Route Authority retains the right to grant similar licences to other digital payment providers on the Route.
- [ ] **Exclusive:** During the Term, the Route Authority shall not grant a similar licence to any competing digital payment platform for the Route, provided Tag-n-Ride maintains the minimum Active Vehicle count specified in Schedule B.

## PART 5: TAG-N-RIDE'S COMPLIANCE WITH NLTA ROUTE CONDITIONS

### 5.1 Regulatory Acknowledgement

Tag-n-Ride acknowledges that:

5.1.1 The Route operates under NLTA authority, and Tag-n-Ride's role is limited to providing digital payment services — Tag-n-Ride does not hold or require a transport operating licence;

5.1.2 Tag-n-Ride shall not direct, control, or interfere with the routing, scheduling, or loading arrangements of Route Operators;

5.1.3 Tag-n-Ride shall comply with any lawful directive from the Department of Transport or relevant transport authority in relation to the deployment of its technology on the Route.

### 5.2 Operational Conditions

Tag-n-Ride undertakes to:

5.2.1 Operate the Platform in a manner compatible with the operating conditions attached to the Route's Operating Permits;

5.2.2 Not deploy Digital Payment Infrastructure at any loading rank or terminal where prohibited by the relevant transport authority;

5.2.3 Provide the Route Authority with advance notice of any planned changes to Platform features that may affect Route Operators.

## PART 6: DATA SHARING WITH THE ROUTE AUTHORITY

### 6.1 Route Data Access

In addition to the Route Access Fee, Tag-n-Ride agrees to provide the Route Authority with anonymised and aggregated route utilisation data as follows:

| Data Type | Format | Frequency |
|---|---|---|
| Total transactions on the Route | Aggregated count | Monthly |
| Total Gross Revenue on the Route | Aggregated ZAR value | Monthly |
| Peak usage periods (time of day / day of week) | Anonymised summary | Monthly |
| Number of Active Vehicles on Route | Count | Monthly |
| Passenger volume estimates | Anonymised | Quarterly |

### 6.2 POPIA Compliance

6.2.1 All data shared with the Route Authority shall be anonymised and shall not contain personal information of individual passengers or Operators, in compliance with the Protection of Personal Information Act 4 of 2013.

6.2.2 The Route Authority shall use route data solely for transport planning, service improvement, and NLTA reporting purposes.

6.2.3 The Route Authority shall not sell, transfer, or commercially exploit the route data provided by Tag-n-Ride.

### 6.3 Ownership

Raw transaction data and Platform data remain the property of Tag-n-Ride. The Route Authority receives a non-exclusive, non-transferable licence to use the anonymised data as permitted under clause 6.2.

## PART 7: TERM AND RENEWAL

### 7.1 Term

This Agreement commences on the Effective Date and continues for [TERM] unless earlier terminated.

### 7.2 Renewal

The Agreement shall automatically renew for successive periods of twelve (12) months unless either Party gives sixty (60) days' written notice of non-renewal before the end of the then-current term.

---

## PART 8: TERMINATION

### 8.1 Termination for Convenience

Either Party may terminate on sixty (60) days' written notice after the first six (6) months of the Term.

### 8.2 Termination for Cause

Either Party may terminate immediately if:

8.2.1 The Route Authority's operating authority for the Route is revoked or suspended;

8.2.2 Tag-n-Ride's payment service authorisation is revoked by a competent authority;

8.2.3 Either Party commits a material breach not remedied within thirty (30) days of notice;

8.2.4 Either Party becomes insolvent.

### 8.3 Consequences

8.3.1 Upon termination, Tag-n-Ride shall deactivate Digital Payment Infrastructure on the Route within seven (7) business days.

8.3.2 Pending settlements for Active Vehicles on the Route shall be processed within thirty (30) days.

8.3.3 Obligations relating to data protection and confidentiality shall survive termination.
