## BACKGROUND

A. Tag-n-Ride operates a digital fare payment platform on minibus taxi routes and in doing so collects anonymised transaction data, route utilisation data, and passenger behaviour data through the operation of the Platform;

B. The Association has an interest in accessing such anonymised data to improve transport planning, monitor route performance, support route authority submissions to the Department of Transport, and enhance member operator services;

C. Tag-n-Ride is willing to share defined categories of anonymised data with the Association, subject to strict data governance obligations and compliance with the Protection of Personal Information Act 4 of 2013 ("**POPIA**") and all applicable privacy laws;

D. This MOU is intended to formalise the data sharing arrangement between the Parties and does not give rise to a binding commercial agreement beyond the obligations specifically recorded herein.

---

## PART 1: PURPOSE

### 1.1 Objectives

The purpose of this MOU is to:

1.1.1 Define the categories of anonymised data that Tag-n-Ride will share with the Association;

1.1.2 Establish governance controls ensuring that shared data is used only for permitted purposes;

1.1.3 Confirm both Parties' POPIA compliance obligations in respect of the shared data;

1.1.4 Prevent the commercial exploitation, resale, or misuse of shared data by either Party;

1.1.5 Establish a framework for responsible data collaboration in the public transport sector.

---

## PART 2: DEFINITIONS

**"Anonymised Data"** means data that has been processed to remove or irreversibly de-identify all personal information of individual passengers, operators, and drivers, such that the data subjects cannot be re-identified from the data alone or in combination with other reasonably available information.

**"Data Governance Framework"** means the policies, procedures, and controls governing the collection, storage, use, sharing, and deletion of data as set out in this MOU.

**"Data Subject"** has the meaning ascribed in POPIA — any natural person whose personal information is being processed.

**"Personal Information"** has the meaning ascribed in section 1 of POPIA.

**"Platform"** means the Tag-n-Ride digital fare payment platform.

**"POPIA"** means the Protection of Personal Information Act 4 of 2013.

**"Route Data"** means anonymised data relating to passenger volumes, trip frequencies, peak usage times, and fare amounts on specific routes, without identifying individual data subjects.

**"Shared Data"** means the specific categories of anonymised data provided by Tag-n-Ride to the Association as defined in Part 3.

**"Unauthorised Use"** means any use of Shared Data that falls outside the Permitted Uses defined in clause 3.3.

---

## PART 3: DATA SHARING FRAMEWORK

### 3.1 Categories of Shared Data

Tag-n-Ride shall provide the Association with the following categories of Shared Data:

| Category | Description | Format |
|---|---|---|
| Route Transaction Volume | Number of transactions per route per day/week/month | Aggregated count |
| Gross Revenue by Route | Total fare value per route per period | Aggregated ZAR |
| Peak Usage Analysis | Peak hours, peak days, seasonal patterns by route | Statistical summary |
| Vehicle Activity | Number of active vehicles per route per period | Aggregated count |
| Passenger Volume Estimates | Estimated number of unique passenger trips | Estimated count |
| Fare Range Analysis | Average, minimum, maximum fare per route | Statistical range |
| Route Comparison | Comparative performance across routes operated by Association members | Index / ranking |

### 3.2 Data Format and Delivery

3.2.1 Shared Data shall be delivered in [PDF report / CSV / Excel / API access] format as agreed between the Parties.

3.2.2 Standard reports shall be provided monthly, within ten (10) business days after the end of each calendar month.

3.2.3 Custom data requests by the Association must be submitted in writing and are subject to Tag-n-Ride's reasonable capacity and the limitations of this MOU.

3.2.4 Tag-n-Ride shall provide a designated data liaison contact for the Association.

### 3.3 Permitted Uses

The Association may use Shared Data solely for the following purposes ("**Permitted Uses**"):

3.3.1 Internal transport planning and route optimisation;

3.3.2 Submissions to the Department of Transport or relevant transport authority in support of route authority renewals or new route applications, provided no single operator's data is identifiable;

3.3.3 Preparation of aggregate performance reports for the Association's executive committee or board;

3.3.4 Member communications presenting anonymised route performance summaries;

3.3.5 Academic or research collaboration, subject to Tag-n-Ride's prior written approval.

### 3.4 Prohibited Uses

The Association is strictly prohibited from:

3.4.1 Selling, licensing, or otherwise commercially exploiting the Shared Data to any third party;

3.4.2 Sharing Shared Data with competing digital payment platform operators;

3.4.3 Attempting to re-identify individual passengers, operators, or drivers from the Shared Data;

3.4.4 Using Shared Data for purposes that are discriminatory, prejudicial, or in contravention of any law;

3.4.5 Making public statements attributing specific commercial performance metrics to individual operators using Shared Data;

3.4.6 Including Shared Data in any public report, media release, or publication without Tag-n-Ride's prior written approval.

## PART 4: DATA GOVERNANCE

### 4.1 Data Governance Controls

The Association undertakes to implement and maintain the following governance controls in relation to Shared Data:

| Control | Requirement |
|---|---|
| Access Control | Only designated Association personnel may access Shared Data |
| Storage Security | Shared Data to be stored on secured systems with appropriate access restrictions |
| Data Inventory | Association to maintain a record of all Shared Data received |
| Retention Limit | Shared Data shall not be retained beyond the Term plus one (1) year |
| Deletion | Upon termination, Association shall delete all Shared Data within 30 days |

### 4.2 Designated Data Recipients

The Association shall designate no more than [NUMBER] named individuals ("**Designated Recipients**") who are authorised to access Shared Data. A list of Designated Recipients shall be provided to Tag-n-Ride on or before the Effective Date and updated within five (5) business days of any change.

### 4.3 Data Incidents

The Association shall notify Tag-n-Ride within 48 hours of becoming aware of any actual or suspected:

4.3.1 Unauthorised access to or use of Shared Data;

4.3.2 Loss, theft, or corruption of Shared Data;

4.3.3 Security compromise affecting systems that store Shared Data.

---

## PART 5: POPIA COMPLIANCE

### 5.1 No Personal Information Transfer

5.1.1 Tag-n-Ride warrants that no personal information (as defined in POPIA) of individual passengers, operators, or drivers will be included in Shared Data.

5.1.2 In the event that Shared Data is subsequently found to contain personal information due to a technical error, the Association must immediately notify Tag-n-Ride and shall not process such information.

### 5.2 Operator Responsibilities

Tag-n-Ride, as the responsible party under POPIA for the Platform data, shall:

5.2.1 Maintain appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect personal information before anonymisation;

5.2.2 Ensure that the anonymisation process meets accepted standards (e.g., k-anonymity or equivalent);

5.2.3 Notify the Information Regulator of any data security compromise in accordance with section 22 of POPIA.

### 5.3 Association's Independent Obligations

5.3.1 The Association remains independently responsible for POPIA compliance in respect of any personal information it holds about its members, and this MOU does not affect those obligations.

5.3.2 The Association shall appoint an Information Officer as required by POPIA (if not already done) and shall process Shared Data in accordance with POPIA conditions for lawful processing.

---

## PART 6: NO RESALE CLAUSE

### 6.1 Absolute Prohibition on Resale

The Association agrees, as an absolute and material condition of this MOU, that it shall not sell, transfer, assign, sublicense, or otherwise commercially exploit the Shared Data or any derivative thereof, to any individual, company, government entity, research institution, or any other person, without the express prior written consent of Tag-n-Ride's CEO.

### 6.2 Consequences of Breach

Any breach of clause 6.1 shall constitute a material breach entitling Tag-n-Ride to:

6.2.1 Immediately terminate this MOU;

6.2.2 Claim damages, including loss of revenue and reputational harm;

6.2.3 Seek an urgent interdict to prevent further exploitation of the Shared Data.

---

## PART 7: INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

7.1 All intellectual property in the Platform, the data collection methodology, and the analytical frameworks used to generate Shared Data remains vested exclusively in Tag-n-Ride.

7.2 This MOU grants the Association only a limited, non-exclusive, non-transferable licence to use Shared Data for Permitted Uses during the Term.

7.3 Any analysis, reports, or derivative works created by the Association using Shared Data shall not be presented as Tag-n-Ride's own analysis without Tag-n-Ride's written approval.

---

## PART 8: TERM AND RENEWAL

### 8.1 Term

This MOU is effective for twelve (12) months from the Effective Date.

### 8.2 Renewal

At the expiry of the Term, the Parties may renew this MOU for further twelve (12)-month periods by written agreement signed by both Parties at least thirty (30) days before expiry.

### 8.3 Review

The Parties shall conduct a joint review of this MOU at month nine (9) to assess performance, consider amendments, and determine whether renewal is appropriate.

---

## PART 9: TERMINATION

### 9.1 Termination for Cause

Either Party may terminate this MOU immediately on written notice if the other Party:

9.1.1 Commits a material breach (including any Unauthorised Use of Shared Data) not remedied within fifteen (15) days of written notice;

9.1.2 Breaches the no-resale clause (clause 6) — no cure period applies.

### 9.2 Termination for Convenience

Either Party may terminate this MOU on thirty (30) days' written notice.

### 9.3 Post-Termination

Upon termination, the Association shall:

9.3.1 Cease accessing or using Shared Data immediately;

9.3.2 Delete or destroy all Shared Data within thirty (30) days;

9.3.3 Provide written certification of deletion to Tag-n-Ride.
