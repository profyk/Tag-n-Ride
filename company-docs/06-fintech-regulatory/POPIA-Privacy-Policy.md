## 5. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

5.1 **Account Management:** To create, verify, and manage your Tag-n-Ride account.

5.2 **Payment Processing:** To facilitate QR-based ride payments, wallet top-ups, withdrawals, and cashouts between passengers, drivers, and fleet owners.

5.3 **KYC/FICA Compliance:** To verify your identity as required by the Financial Intelligence Centre Act ("FICA") and our obligation as a financial services platform.

5.4 **Fraud Detection:** To detect, prevent, and investigate suspicious transactions or account activity.

5.5 **Customer Support:** To respond to queries, complaints, and disputes.

5.6 **Regulatory Compliance:** To meet our obligations to the FSCA, SARB, SARS, and other regulators.

5.7 **Communications:** To send transactional notifications (payment confirmations, cashout alerts), service updates, and — with your consent — marketing messages.

5.8 **Platform Improvement:** To analyse usage patterns and improve our products and services.

## 6. HOW WE SHARE YOUR INFORMATION

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only as follows:

| Recipient | Reason |
|-----------|--------|
| **Stitch** (payment processor) | Processing payments and bank transfers |
| **Hosting providers** (Railway, Vercel) | Platform operation |
| **SMS/notification providers** | Sending alerts and communications |
| **Regulators** (FSCA, SARB, SARS, FIC) | Legal obligations |
| **Law enforcement** | When required by court order or law |
| **Auditors and legal advisors** | Company compliance and legal matters |
| **Counterparty to your transaction** | Limited payment details (e.g., driver sees that payment was received) |

All third-party processors are required to maintain appropriate data security and may not use your data for their own purposes.

## 10. SECURITY

10.1 We implement industry-standard technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information, including:
  (a) Encryption of data in transit (TLS/HTTPS) and at rest;
  (b) Access controls and role-based permissions;
  (c) Two-factor authentication for administrative access;
  (d) Regular security assessments.

10.2 Despite our measures, no system is 100% secure. In the event of a data breach, we will notify affected individuals and the Information Regulator as required by POPIA within **72 hours**.
